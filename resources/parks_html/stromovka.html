Introduction: What to do in Prague: Prague parks: Stromovka

Stromovka

Recently, the declared park has been heard mainly in connection with two accidents: a fire in the left part of the Industrial Palace and a landslide due to the excavation of part of the city ring tunnel. The park is very popular among Prague residents, but also visitors to the capital, and therefore these events attracted a lot of attention.

  • Linda Nová, portal editor
  • Photo gallery

The point is that Stromovka, also called the Royal Game Reserve, could be considered a kind of Central Park of Prague. A large green oasis with water areas, mature trees, under which there is a pleasant shade, the possibility of organizing picnics or family sports competitions, a place for walks for mothers with prams as well as for seniors. On the one hand accessible directly from tram stations, on the other hand even from a steamer. It traverses the northern edge of the park, and either you can head directly to Stromovka, or to the opposite side across the footbridge towards Troja and the ZOO.

Základní info

Refreshments: Staročeská rychta restaurant at the Exhibition Grounds

WC: In adjacent restaurants

Attractions: For families with children and tourists, possible walk to the ZOO and Botanical Gardens, dogs yes, natural attractions, Planetarium, Exhibition Grounds

Děti a sport

Bike paths: YES

Skating: YES

Sports field: In the vicinity of the TK Sparta Prague complex - tennis courts

Playground: YES 2x

Kudy do parku

Public transport: Exhibition grounds, tram. No. 5, 12, 14, 15, 17, 53, 54, Sibiřské nám., bus No. 131, by steamer from Rašínov nábř.

GPS location: 50˚ 6 '27.80 "N 14˚ 25' 12.53" E

Parking: Next to the Exhibition Grounds or in Bubeneč

Doplňkové info

Road surface: Reinforced threshing and asphalt

Operator: Capital City of Prague

Opening hours: Free access all year round
